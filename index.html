Dragon tree

The dragon tree in the picture is a large, wild version of the spikey, tropical house plant you might find in any given living room.

Bristlecone pine

These trees are long-lived and resilient in harsh weather and bad soils. Some in the US Southwest are more than 4000 years old.

Boabab tree

The boabab tree is native to Madagascar and grows in South Africa. One Baobab tree in Zimbabwe is so large it can shelter 40 people in its trunk.

Madrone tree

Madrones are native to the Pacific Northwest and tend to grow close to the water along coastlines. Their twisted trunks and branches are covered in a thin red bark that peels.

Japanese Maple

This tree has smaller leaves than a typical maple. It is known for its vibrant leaf and stem colors making it stand out throughout the growing season.
